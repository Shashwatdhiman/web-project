Why Exercise is Important

Regular exercise is one of the best things you can do for your body and mind. It helps improve cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and supports mental well-being.

It boosts energy levels, enhances mood, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.

Making exercise a part of your routine leads to a healthier, happier, and more productive life.
